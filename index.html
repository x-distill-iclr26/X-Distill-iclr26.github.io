Abstract

This is where you paste your paper's abstract. Knowledge distillation aims to transfer knowledge from a large, sophisticated teacher model to a smaller, more efficient student model while preserving performance. Our approach, X-Distill, investigates a direct and computationally efficient paradigm by aligning the final global feature representations of a ViT teacher and a CNN student. This simpler approach aims to imbue a computationally efficient CNN with the powerful, holistic understanding of a large Transformer, providing a highly effective initialization for downstream robotic manipulation tasks.
